About

The DesignerAladdin Vicuna

A professional game level and quest designer with experience shipping titles across multiple genres — from large-scale MMOs and open-world ARPGs to horror, racing, and simulation. Adept at taking a level from whitebox to fully dressed environment, designing quest flow and narrative beats, and collaborating across disciplines.

Comfortable working in Unreal Engine, Unity, and proprietary pipelines. Has contributed to projects at Shiba Inu, Lost Boys Interactive, Consortium9, Jade Stem Studios, Pineapple Studios, and Spitfire Games.
